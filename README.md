# The Tarot Detective — Player Feedback

> *"Everyone lies. The trick is figuring out what they're lying about and why."*
> — Vera Saint-Claire

---

You made it through Crescent City. Maybe you found the truth. Maybe it found you.

Either way, you have opinions. We want to hear them.

**Arcana Investigations** is a noir detective game built on tarot mechanics and player choice. It is hand-crafted, independently made, and still in active development. Your feedback shapes what comes next.

---

## How to Submit Feedback

Click **[New Issue](https://github.com/thetarotdetective/feedback/issues/new/choose)** and pick the template that fits:

| Template | Use it for |
|---|---|
| **Narrative Feedback** | Story beats, dialogue, character reactions, choice weight |
| **Atmosphere Note** | Visuals, audio, UI, the overall vibe of a scene |
| **Bug Report** | Something broke, a choice led nowhere, text displayed wrong |

No GitHub account? Drop feedback at [our itch.io page](https://stackbilt-admin.itch.io/arcana-investigations) in the comments.

---

## Free Key Offer

Submit a meaningful issue (any template) and we will send you a free key for the next release.

*Meaningful means more than one sentence. Tell us what you felt, what confused you, what landed.*

Reply to your issue with your itch.io username and we will get it to you.

## A Note on the Cards

The tarot in this game is not decoration. Every draw is a narrative tool — it reframes the scene, tilts the odds, opens a door that would otherwise stay shut. If a card moment did not land for you, that is important to know.
